WHO WE ARE

Kijiji is an up and coming brand in the region and the real estate space. With our cutting edge technologies and modern philosophies, we are based upon securing of customers needs and wants with the least challenge possible and minimal risk applied.

Kijiji has a group of 6 founding members who also serve as co-presidents with a joint and united mission in being to ensure a positive environment for all staff, customers and most importantly stakeholders through our offering of cutting-edge products and services.

While we may be a young company, we bring a youthful exuberance with modern inspirations that the older spaces in the sector cannot boast of. That is while still maintaining levels of experience and competence.

WHY US

Kijiji offers you affordable real estate solutions at minimal inconvenience and at suitable rates and payment plans. We also:

  1. We ensure that our properties have value sustainance and addition over time.
  2. Excellent customer service and support with matter relating to the property even after purchase.
  3. We ensure that all required documents such as title deeds are correctly and promptly furnished.
  4. Through our youth policy, we ensure job creation for youth in our region.
